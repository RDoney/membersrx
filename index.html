An Onboarding Tool for Self Funded Plans

Administrators & plan sponsors are learning that higher cost does not equal better quality care. MembersRX uses modern technology to teach members how to use their plan to access the best care possible for a lower price.

EASY TO USE

Member engagement is hard. MembersRX presents plan information in an easy-to-understand way, at a time when members are actually paying attention to their health insurance.

CUSTOMIZED FOR YOUR PLAN

Every employee population is unique. Your software is specific to your plan, and provides an intuitive walkthrough of the programs prescribed by plan administrators.

TRACK USER PROGRESS

Education is critical to the success of your self funded plan. Real-time analytics show what programs members are understanding well, and what concepts may need further education.

How Does It Work?

MembersRX teaches members what their plan provides, and how to use it to navigate a complex healthcare marketplace.

We analyze the range of cost savings programs you offer as an administrator.

Each program is developed into an individual chapter. When you prescribe that program to a client, we plug that chapter into their onboarding course.

Intuitive for Users and Admins

At renewal time, new members can access their onboarding couse using only their name and group number.

Whether you're an administrator, plan sponsor, or broker, you can track how your participants are progressing through the program.

Through analytics and heatmap data, we can gain insight into what's working and what needs improvement.
